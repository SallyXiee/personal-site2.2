This map shows median home values in the U.S. at state and county levels, using data from the 2021 ACS 5-Year Estimates (B25077_001E). I chose this dataset to explore housing affordability and regional disparities in home prices, where coastal states tend to have higher prices, and urban areas contrast sharply with rural regions.

To ensure a balanced visualization, I used quantile classification, which evenly distributes data into five groups. This prevents extreme values from dominating the map, making patterns clearer and easier to interpret.
